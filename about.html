About Crimson Banner Studios

Our Mission

At Crimson Banner Studios, we believe in the power of games to transport players to magical worlds and create unforgettable experiences. We're dedicated to crafting indie games that prioritize innovation, creativity, and player engagement.

Our Story

Founded by a passionate team of game developers and fantasy enthusiasts, Crimson Banner Studios emerged from a shared vision: to create games that blend strategic depth with immersive storytelling. We started with a love for chess, magic, and fantasy worlds, channeling that passion into our flagship title, Wizard's Chess: Wizard's Gambit.

Our Values

Quality First

Every line of code, every asset, every design decision reflects our commitment to excellence.

Player Focus

We design with players in mind, creating engaging experiences that resonate and inspire.

Creative Vision

We push boundaries and challenge conventions to deliver unique gaming experiences.

Our Team

We're a small but mighty team of indie developers, artists, and designers who are passionate about creating extraordinary games. Each member brings unique skills and perspectives, united by our love of fantasy, strategy, and compelling gameplay. We work collaboratively, iterate rapidly, and never stop learning.

Looking Forward

As we grow, our ambition remains clear: to establish Crimson Banner Studios as a beacon of independent game development excellence. With several projects in development and new ideas constantly brewing, we're excited about the future. We invite you to join us on this journey through magical worlds and epic adventures.
